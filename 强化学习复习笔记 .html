MRP

  • ss′=ℙ[St+1=s′|St=s]

  • s=𝔼[Rt+1|St=s]

  • return Gt=Rt+1+γRt+2+...=∑∞k=0γkRt+k+1

  • v(s)=𝔼[Gt|St=s]

  • Bellman Equation for MRPs

    v(s)=𝔼[Gt|St=s]=𝔼[Rt+1+γRt+2+γ2Rt+3+…|St=s]=𝔼[Rt+1+γ∑k=0∞γkRt+k+2|St=s]=s+γ𝔼∑k=0∞γkRt+k+2|St=s]=s+γ𝔼∑s′ss′𝔼[∑k=0∞γkRt+k+2|St=s,St+1=s′]=s+γ𝔼∑s′ss′𝔼[∑k=0∞γkRt+k+2|St+1=s′]=s+γ∑s′ss′v(s′)

    ⎡⎣⎢⎢⎢v(1)⋮v(n)⎤⎦⎥⎥⎥=⎡⎣⎢⎢1⋮n⎤⎦⎥⎥+⎡⎣⎢⎢11,…,1n⋮n1,…,nn⎤⎦⎥⎥⎡⎣⎢⎢v(1)⋮v(n)⎤⎦⎥⎥

    i.e.
    v=+γv

MDP

  • ass′=ℙ[St+1=s′|St=s′,At=s′]

  • as=𝔼[Rt+1|St=s,At=a]

  • A policy π is a distribution over actions given states,

    π(a|s)=ℙ(At=a|St=s)

  • πss′=∑a∈Aπ(a|s)ass′

  • πs=∑a∈Aπ(a|s)as

  • vπ(s)=𝔼π[Gt|St=s]

  • qπ(a,s)=𝔼[Gt+1|St=s,At=a]

Bellman Expectation Equation

vπ(s)=∑a∈Aπ(a|s)qπ(s,a)

qπ(s,a)=as+γ∑s′ass′vπ(s′)

Bellman Expectation Equation for vπ:

vπ(s)=∑a∈Aπ(a|s)(Ras+γ∑s′∈Sass′vπ(s′))

Bellman Expectation Equation for qπ:
qπ(s,a)=as+γ∑s′∈Sass′∑a′∈Aπ(s′|a′)qπ(s′,a′)

Matrix Form:

vπ=π+γvπ
